Aries

3 Famous people: James Franco, Robert Downey JR., Reese Witherspoon.

There is so much excitement and fun waiting for you just around the corner. Still, you are unable to see it for now and need to accept the fact that sadness and loneliness need to be faced and let go instead of pushing them under the rug. Soon enough, Mars will come to your doorstep and your energy will return. If you pay close attention and sense the atmosphere of what is approaching, you could even recognize it sneaking up behind your back.
